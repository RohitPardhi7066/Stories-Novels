The crow tried to push his head into the jug. sadly he found that the neck of the jug was to narrow. Then he tried to ouch te jug to tiltfor the water to flow out, but the jug was to heavy.

The crow tought hard for a while. Then, looking around it,he saw some pebbles one by one, dropping each into the jug, As more and more pebbles filled the jug, the water level kept rising. Soon it was high enough for the crow to drink. His plan had worked!
